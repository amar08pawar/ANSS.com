1. Alcohol Classification:
Alcohols with an sp³ C-OH bond are classified as primary, secondary, or tertiary based on how many carbon atoms are attached to the carbon bearing the OH group.
2. Allylic & Benzylic Alcohols:
Allylic alcohols have the OH group attached to a carbon next to a C=C double bond. Benzylic alcohols are next to an aromatic ring.
